Our Services

We have many collection points in Zimbabwe. Just check out on the drop down menu for your town. You can collect at any of these points while you are in Zimbabwe. If we are yet to open a point in your city, ping us. The city with most requests gets the collection point faster.

You can also receive money from Zimbabwe through us. We have colection points in South Africa too, though they are few. We do not leave out those without bank accounts, you can come to our offices as of old times and send money to your loved ones.
