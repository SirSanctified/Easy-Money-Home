About Us

Easy Money Home has been around for three years now serving both those in South Africa and Zimbabwe. We have built relationships with our clients and fullfilled our promises. At the heart of our culture is committment to perfection, providing the best services our clients needs. Our company was proudly founded by Zimbabweans and is run by Zimbabweans, your trusted homeboys.

Vision

Our vision is to create a better everyday life for many people.

Mission

A team of professionals committed to providing insight to our partners through innovation, efficiency and transparency while maintaining fiscal integrity.

Our Values

Honesty

We are open to our customers and expect them to be open us too. These open conversations not only build strong relationships, but they create opportunities for us to truly help customers.

Reliability

We strive to facilitate results that are significant, consistent and effective to our customers.

Why Do We Exist?

We serve as an intermediary between senders and recipients of money between Zimbabwe and South Africa, and execute transactions involving cash, that was somewhat impossible to do in a secure way in the past. Not only do we exist to make this secure, but to make it easier as well as instant and seemless.
